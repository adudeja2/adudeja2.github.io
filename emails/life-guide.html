1. Key Person Life Insurance Basics
Key person life insurance, also known as “key man” insurance, presents great opportunities for advisors and agents.

Companies use key person insurance to protect themselves against the loss of the most important executives. When an agent is working with a corporate client, they often have an opportunity to sell one or more key person policies for executives within a company — multiple sales for about the same amount of work as for a single sale.

Key person insurance is most frequently bought to protect partners in a business if one of them dies. It can also be used to fund a buy-sell agreement.
