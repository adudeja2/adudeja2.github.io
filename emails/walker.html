​​Walker & Dunlop, the #3 multifamily finance lender in the U.S.*, has been a major player in the affordable housing space for years, and we are committed to expanding our presence in the market by providing our clients with unparalleled solutions for all their affordable housing needs. With our acquisition of Alliant Capital and the addition of a dedicated affordable investment sales team, our affordable capabilities have grown significantly over the past year.

Our full suite of services, unmatched affordable housing experience, exceptional expertise in transaction execution, and industry-leading technology are setting a new standard in affordable housing.

Our collaborative team works together to ensure our clients receive the best advice, service, and execution at every step of the property lifecycle, and we take pride in empowering our clients to create, preserve, and revitalize communities where people can afford to live, work, shop, and play. Visit our website to learn more about the broad capabilities of our affordable platform.
