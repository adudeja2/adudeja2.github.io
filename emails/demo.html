THREE CRITICAL FACILITIES QUESTIONS TO ASK YOURSELF FOR 2022

…AND ANSWERS FROM THE DESIGN/BUILD EXPERT

As the calendar turns over to the new year, and your strategic plans kick into gear, don't forget to include your facilities in your considerations for 2022. Sometimes our focus is clear when it comes to member service, loan goals, new products, technology advancements and other front-office strategies, However, our facilities themselves are the key link to service delivery, and that link undoubtedly looks different than it did 18 months ago.

The first question to ask yourself is:

How much of my pandemic member service transition will be permanently moved to digital channels and remote service?

Depending on your analytics and staff assessment, it may be critical to redefine your overall footprint and “hub and spoke” service delivery model. Don’t have analytics or confused on where to begin? Tap into your experts at DEI. Our process starts with a market and channel delivery assessment to provide clear answers and a path forward.

As you move forward through the assessment discussion, the next question is:

How can our facilities more efficiently deliver member service?

Determining the path your members take will lead you through your online tools, website, call center and finally through your branch network and individual branch locations. As we interact less frequently in person, maximizing the member interactions we do have becomes critical. Rethinking your branch configuration and potentially remodeling locations to create efficiency and more subject matter expert interaction will lead you forward.

And finally:

Do we have the right mix of physical locations in the right places?

This is the million-dollar question that will drive your market success across your entire footprint—whether in one city, multiple cities, or even multiple states! Your DEI expert team will lead your strategic discussions and help draft a roadmap of purposeful effort and evaluation for your entire branch network. Reimagining your connecting points, locations and physical branch strategy will be key to sustaining a formidable market presence.

With 2022 days away, the time to think ahead is now. Your members await your efficient, expert-driven solutions to meet their evolving needs.
