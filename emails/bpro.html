Level-funded plans from BlueCross now in Catoosa, Dade and Walker counties in GA.

BlueCross BlueShield of Tennessee has arrived for small businesses in Catoosa, Dade and Walker counties in Georgia.

With our level-funded plans designed for companies with 5 to 150 employees, you can now offer your clients predictable, affordable health plans, while you get an experienced BlueCross team to support you.

Level funding offers your clients one consistent monthly payment, regardless of actual monthly claims, along with lower taxes. And at the end of the year, if their total incurred claims costs are lower than their claims funding payments, they’ll share in a surplus credit upon renewing.
