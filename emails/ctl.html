3 social media marketing tips

Roughly 70% of Americans use social media, according to the Pew Research Center. If you don’t use social media, you may be missing opportunities to connect with potential residents.

These three tips can help establish and amplify a social presence for your property that could lead to business results.

  1. Choose your social media platforms wisely
    Before you join TikTok, Instagram or any other channel, ask yourself a few questions:
    • Who’s your audience?
    • What are you trying to achieve?
    • How much time and budget do you have?
  2. Invest in high-quality imagery
    Your photos and videos don’t need to be perfect, but they do need to be high quality. If you aren’t able to hire professionals, brush up on your photography or videography skills via free online courses. And make use of editing tools and filters offered by many platforms to enhance your visuals.
  3. Stay active
    “Oftentimes, social media marketing is trial and error, so don’t get discouraged,” Rankin said. Instead, look at metrics to see what’s performing well and what isn’t. “What types of images are your audience engaging with the most?”

    As you continue your social media journey, spend time researching trends and following other profiles and influencers in your space for inspiration.
