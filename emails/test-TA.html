Employers sponsoring retirement plans provide their employees with a valued savings vehicle. Offering this benefit means becoming the fiduciary for the plan and being responsible for compliance with IRS and DOL regulations, maintaining accurate employee data, complying with notification delivery deadlines, and selecting service providers and investments.

Employers might not be aware that help is available. Join this complimentary webcast to learn more about how to help plan sponsors assess their options. We’ll cover:
•   The different types of fiduciaries, what they provide and what they don’t
•   The driving forces for 3(16) Fiduciary Services in today’s business environment
•   How to assess when and how 3(16) Fiduciary Services are an appropriate option for plan sponsor clients
